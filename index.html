Our Story

A food scientist quit her job to give options of healthy plant based foods with a request to all:

Do yourself a favor, eat healthy live longer

Our Team

Simply Vegan Inc is a female and minority-owned company started by a Certified Food Scientist with over 30 years of experience in food safety and quality management. She is a PCQI (preventive Control Qualified Individual) and is HACCP certified. Following completion of MS in Botany (study of plants), her passion for food led her to also do MS in Food Science from the University of Alberta, Canada. She is highly motivated for vegan food manufacturing because she believes she can help people lead healthy lives.

Like Minded individuals supported her in this mission to manufacture healthy plant-based food items. For us this is a movement more than just a business.

Benefits of
Plant Based Foods

Vegan (Plant Based) foods provide:

  • Plant Proteins
  • Antioxidants
  • Fiber
  • And many more phytochemicals

Plant based foods are high in fiber and have no cholesterol where as animal based foods contain cholesterol and no fiber. Thus, plant based foods should be a part of all diets, not just vegan ones.

Ingredients

Simply Vegan will prepare products with simple time tested ingredients. We do not choose ingredients based on how hard or easy it is to pronounce. We use ingredients that has been used for years.

For example, Asafetida is so hard to pronounce but is a very beneficial ingredient used in eastern culture for centuries in cooking especially beans. This is also used in Ayurveda as medicine to release gas.
